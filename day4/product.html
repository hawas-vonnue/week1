About the Project

Mjolnir is the hammer of the thunder god Thor in Norse universe,used both as a devasting weapon and as a divine instrument to provide blessings.The hammer was commonly worn as a pendent during Viking Age in the Scandinavian culuture sphere,and Thos and his hammer occur on variety of objects. Mjolnir is enchanted hammer. Mjolnir also has a secret name with it comes which can be used to call the hammer to the users.Although speaking the name aloud is not required,only an act of will,the person must actively remember the name to call it.There is also Helnir made the dark opposite of Mjolnir,twinning them through time and space.

Properties

Mjolnir is crafted from a nugget of Uru,a poweful nigh-invulnerable Asgardian metal. Additional enchantments made it virtually indestructable. It can withstand heat as extreme as the heart of suns,and blasts of powerful enough to destroy planets. Almost nothing is capable of withstanding a blow from Mjolnir,not even the armor of the powerful Celestials.

Worthiness Enchantment

One of the hammer's most notable attributes is its worthiness enchantment.This spell surrounds Mjolnir adn prevents it from being wielded by anyone save those are its owners. To anyone else,Mjolnir can't be lifted from the ground,as if it were immeasurably heavy even though its actual weight is about 42.3 pounds.

Return to its wielder's hand enchantment

Mjolnir has also been enchanted so that it will always return to its owner.Mjolnir and its owner share a mystical link that allows the latter to command the hammer. if the owner's will is strong enough, the hammer can pass through nearly any barrier to reach them should they choose.

Transformation of its wielder's body enchantment

Due to an enchantment we placed on it Mjolnir is able to transform owner between his godly form and his mortal form,with hammer usually assuming the form of an old wooden cane or one of the owners choice. Another provision of this enchantment required that the user could not be out of physical contact with Mjolnir for more than sixty seconds without spontaneously reverting to his mortal form.
