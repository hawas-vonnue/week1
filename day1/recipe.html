Carrot Cake Cookies

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
    • or Gluten free flour
  • Baking soda
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • spices
  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Carrots
  • Cream cheese
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Pecans

Method steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree. Line two 18 by 13-in baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl whisk together flour,baking soda, baking powder,salt,cinnamon,ginger and nutmeg for 20s ,set aside.
  3. In the large mixing bowl using an electric hand mixer blend together butter,granulated sugar and brown sugar until creamy.
  4. Blend in egg and vanilla extract.
  5. Add half of the flour mixture and mix just until combined, then mix in carrots, then mix in remaining 1/2 of the flour mixture just until combined. Scrape sides and bottom of bowl with a spatula to make sure everything is properly incorporated.
  6. Scoop dough out with a medium 1 1/2 tbsp cookie scoop and drop onto prepared baking sheets spacing cookies 2-inches apart
  7. Bake preheated oven until set,about 12m. Cool on baking sheet several minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool. After 10m of cooling on rack finish cooling completely in airtight container so they dont dry. Once cool, frost with cream cheese frosting and top with pecans if desired.
  8. In a mixing bowl using an electir hand mixer whip together cream cheese, butter ,powdered sugar and vanilla on low speed blended. Increase to high speed and whip several minutes until light and fluffy. If doing the carrot decoration set aside a few tbsp to ting green and a few tablespoons to tint orange with food coloring. Add to a piping bag set with a small round tip and decorate.
